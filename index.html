The A4 Lab (Actions against Advanced Adversaries) at University of Arizona is directed by Professor Zhuolin Yang. We research at the intersection of Security, Usability, and Artificial Intelligence (AI). In a world of ever-advancing adversaries (now armed with powerful AI models and computing systems), we aim to build usable tools that help individuals maintain agency over their security and privacy.
